Project Plans

Project Statement and Purpose

My project site would be to recreate/continue working on the MonMon Sushi Restaurant project and the target audience for my site would be the people who are craving for fresh Sushi. In short, customers! The other goal that I have for my project site is to provide great user experience by making it easier to navigate through and make it easy for people who are using their mobile phones to browse my site. The purpose of my project site is to start creating the website that would be used in the future for the sushi restaurant that we are planning to establish.

Site Outline

The site outline would follow a simple e-commerce/restaurant type of site.

  1. Home Page
    • Hero Image
    • Short intro about MonMon Sushi Restaurant
    • Category of foods (sushi, drinks, sides and desserts) which includes a button to view the full menu
    • Why MonMon's Sushi?
    • Store Hours
  2. Gallery
    • Pictures of food/meals
  3. Menu
    • Lists of available food/drinks with visuals of what they look
  4. Contact Page
    • Includes a list of our contact information including our phone number
